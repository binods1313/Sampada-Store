## Who We Are {#who-we-are}

Sampada Originals is an online store operated by Binod S, selling premium Indian fashion and lifestyle products at sampadaoriginals.in. You can reach us at binods1313@gmail.com.

## Information We Collect {#information-we-collect}

We collect information you provide directly — your name, email address, shipping address, and phone number when you create an account or place an order. We also collect order history, payment confirmation data (we do not store full card details), and messages you send to our support team.

We automatically collect device and browser data, IP address, pages visited, and session duration through cookies and analytics tools.

## How We Use Your Information {#how-we-use-it}

We use your data to process and fulfil orders, send shipping updates, respond to support queries, detect fraud, improve our website experience, and comply with applicable Indian law. We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

## Google Sign-In {#google-signin}

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We use this only to create and manage your Sampada account. We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, or any other Google services.

## Third-Party Processors {#third-parties}

We share data only with vendors necessary to operate our business — including our payment gateway (Stripe), hosting provider (Vercel), email delivery service (SendGrid), and analytics platform (Google Analytics). Each processor is bound by their own data protection terms.

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies {#cookies}

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your cart. We use analytics cookies to understand site usage. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to shop with us.

## Data Retention {#data-retention}

We retain your account and order data for as long as your account is active and for up to seven years afterward to meet legal and tax obligations under Indian law. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

## Your Rights {#your-rights}

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing binods1313@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

## Security {#security}

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) and access controls to protect your data. No system is perfectly secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take all reasonable precautions.

## Children's Privacy {#children}

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us immediately.

## International Transfers {#international}

Your data is stored and processed in India. If you access our site from outside India, your data may be transferred to and processed within India in accordance with applicable law.

## Changes to This Policy {#changes}

We will post any updates to this page and revise the Last Updated date above. Continued use of our site after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy. For questions, email binods1313@gmail.com.
