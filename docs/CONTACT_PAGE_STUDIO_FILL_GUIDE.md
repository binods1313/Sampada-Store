### 💡 Tips for Contact Cards:

- **Icon Emoji**: Use single emoji characters (📍, 📱, ⏰, 📧, 💬, 🏢, etc.)
- **Card Title**: Keep it short (2-3 words max)
- **Content**: Use line breaks for readability
- **Link**: 
  - For phone: `tel:+919876543210` (no spaces)
  - For email: `mailto:email@example.com`
  - For maps: Full Google Maps URL
  - For websites: `https://example.com`
  - Leave empty if no link needed

1. Go to Google Maps

3. Click "Share"
4. Click "Embed a map"
5. Copy the URL from the iframe src attribute
6. Paste here

### Form Functionality:

3. Show success/error messages as configured
4. Send data to your email (if email integration configured)

## 🎨 Design Tips

### Hero Image:
- Use warm, inviting photos of your studio or workspace
- Show artisans at work or beautiful textile close-ups
- Ensure good contrast for text overlay readability

### Contact Cards:
- Keep content concise and scannable
- Use clear, recognizable emoji icons
- Group related information together

### FAQs:
- Address real customer questions
- Keep answers under 100 words
- Organize by category for better navigation
