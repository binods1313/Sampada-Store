## 📖 OUR STORY

### **Our Story Title**
```
The Sampada Story
```

---

### **Story Content**
```
Sampada began with a simple belief: that clothing should be more than fabric and thread. It should be a story, a legacy, a piece of art you wear.

Founded in 2024 in the heart of Bengaluru, we set out to bridge the gap between India's rich textile heritage and modern design sensibilities. Our founders, inspired by the elegance of traditional Indian attire and the craftsmanship of artisan communities, envisioned a brand that would honor the past while dressing the future.

Every Sampada piece is designed with intention. We work closely with skilled artisans, use ethically sourced fabrics, and ensure that every stitch reflects our commitment to quality. From our signature heritage collections to contemporary everyday wear, each garment tells a story of care, culture, and craftsmanship.

Today, Sampada is more than a clothing brand — it's a movement. A celebration of slow fashion, mindful consumption, and the timeless beauty of well-made clothing. We're building a community of people who value quality over quantity, heritage over hype, and style that lasts.

This is just the beginning. Join us as we write the next chapter of Indian fashion — one garment, one story, one legacy at a time.
```

## 🎨 LOGO VISIBILITY GUIDELINES

When creating or selecting images for the Company Page:

### **Logo Placement Options**:
1. **On Clothing**: Logo visible on garment tags, labels, or embroidered on fabric
2. **Backdrop/Wall**: Large logo display on studio wall or backdrop
3. **Product Tags**: Close-up shots showing Sampada logo on product tags
4. **Watermark**: Subtle logo watermark in corner (bottom right or top left)
5. **Center Focus**: Logo as main focal point with products arranged around it

### **Logo Visibility Checklist**:
- [ ] Logo is clearly visible and not obscured
- [ ] Logo colors (gold and green) are accurate and vibrant
- [ ] Sanskrit "स" symbol is sharp and legible
- [ ] Leaf mandala pattern is detailed and clear
- [ ] Logo is well-lit (not in shadow)
- [ ] Logo size is appropriate (not too small or overwhelming)
- [ ] Logo placement feels natural and branded

### **Color Coordination**:
- **Logo colors**: Gold (#C9A96E) and Green (emerald/forest green)
- **Match these colors** in clothing, props, and lighting
- **Complementary tones**: Warm earth tones, cream, beige, dark navy
- **Avoid**: Colors that clash with gold/green (bright red, neon colors)
