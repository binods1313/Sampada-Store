## ❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### FAQ Section Title
```
Frequently Asked Questions
```

### FAQ Description
```
Find quick answers to common questions about orders, shipping, returns, and our heritage collections.
```

### FAQ #1
- **Question:** `How long does shipping take?`
- **Answer:** `We offer express shipping across India with delivery in 3-5 business days for metro cities and 5-7 business days for other locations. International shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination.`
- **Category:** Shipping

### FAQ #2
- **Question:** `What is your return and exchange policy?`
- **Answer:** `We offer a 7-day easy return and exchange policy. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags intact. Custom-made and sale items are non-returnable. Visit our Returns page for detailed instructions.`
- **Category:** Returns

### FAQ #3
- **Question:** `How do I track my order?`
- **Answer:** `Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.`
- **Category:** Orders

### FAQ #4
- **Question:** `Do you offer international shipping?`
- **Answer:** `Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. All customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the customer.`
- **Category:** Shipping

### FAQ #5
- **Question:** `How do I know my size?`
- **Answer:** `Each product page includes a detailed size chart. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing with our size guide. For personalized sizing assistance, contact our support team via WhatsApp or email.`
- **Category:** Products

### FAQ #6
- **Question:** `Are your products authentic Indian heritage designs?`
- **Answer:** `Absolutely! Every Sampada piece celebrates authentic Indian craftsmanship and heritage. We work with skilled artisans to create designs that honor traditional techniques while embracing contemporary style.`
- **Category:** Products

### FAQ #7
- **Question:** `Can I customize or personalize my order?`
- **Answer:** `Yes, we offer customization services for select products. Contact our team at support@sampada.com with your requirements, and we'll guide you through the process and pricing.`
- **Category:** General

### FAQ #8
- **Question:** `What payment methods do you accept?`
- **Answer:** `We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, PayPal, Razorpay, and cash on delivery (COD) for orders within India. All transactions are secure and encrypted.`
- **Category:** Payment

### FAQ #9
- **Question:** `How do I care for my Sampada garments?`
- **Answer:** `Each product comes with specific care instructions on the label. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning for embellished pieces and gentle hand wash for cotton items. Avoid direct sunlight when drying to preserve colors.`
- **Category:** Products

### FAQ #10
- **Question:** `Do you have a physical store?`
- **Answer:** `Currently, Sampada operates exclusively online to bring you the best prices and widest selection. However, we occasionally host pop-up events in major cities. Follow us on social media for updates.`
- **Category:** General
